About the Project

Our project focuses on creating biodegradable gloves from cassava starch, offering a sustainable alternative to traditional plastic gloves. This innovation supports SDG 9 by promoting industry innovation and environmental sustainability.

By utilizing cassava, a renewable resource, we aim to reduce the environmental impact of disposable gloves. Our gloves decompose naturally, minimizing waste and pollution. This project not only addresses the pressing issue of plastic waste but also encourages the adoption of sustainable practices across industries.

We are committed to fostering innovation and infrastructure development, aligning with global efforts to combat climate change and promote a healthier planet. Join us in our mission to revolutionize the glove industry with eco-friendly solutions.
